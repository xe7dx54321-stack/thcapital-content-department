# P26-003 Multi-lane Hot Signal Capture Report

## Goal

Capture hot signals from multiple local artifact lanes instead of relying only on the official lane.

## Lanes

Official AI lab, model release, agent framework, open source, paper research, AI infra, funding/startup, developer community, China AI media, global AI media, and social weak signal.

## Boundary

Signals are derived from local manifests and topic candidates only. Empty lanes are explicitly marked `EMPTY` or `MISSING_INPUT`.
