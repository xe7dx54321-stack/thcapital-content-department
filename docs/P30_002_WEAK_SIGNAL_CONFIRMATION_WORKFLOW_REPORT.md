# P30-002 Weak Signal Confirmation Workflow Report

## Goal

Classify OpenClaw migrated weak signals into confirmable, needs-primary-source, needs-second-source, manual-review, watch, and blocked states.

## Outputs

- `latest_weak_signal_confirmation_workflow.json`
- `latest_weak_signal_confirmation_workflow.md`
- `latest_weak_signal_confirmation_workflow_board.md`

## Boundary

Weak signals remain weak until confirmed by stronger sources. Reddit, X, YouTube, heat, and WeChat signals cannot become hard evidence through this workflow.
