# Output Contract

The optimizer should produce outputs that an operator can immediately use.

## 1. Reusable learnings

A reusable learning should be:

- specific
- conditional
- tied to evidence
- easy to apply next round

Bad:

- `Need better titles`

Good:

- `When the topic is a niche company, the company or object must be named by paragraph 2 on WeChat, otherwise click-through may not convert into understanding.`

## 2. Next-round experiments

Every experiment should include:

- what to change
- where to change it
- what outcome to watch

Example:

- `Change`: use a result-first title instead of a company-name-first title
- `Where`: WeChat packaging layer
- `Watch`: click-through and first-screen retention comments

## 3. Skill write-back mapping

The optimizer should name the layer that needs updating:

- `th-market-hook-title-cover`
- `th-market-context-bridge`
- `th-market-audience-translator`
- `th-market-platform-renderer`
- `th-topic-radar`
