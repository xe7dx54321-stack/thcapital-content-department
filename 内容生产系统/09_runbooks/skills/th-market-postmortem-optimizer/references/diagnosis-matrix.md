# Diagnosis Matrix

Map symptoms to likely causes before proposing fixes.

## 1. Low click + low read-through

Possible causes:

- weak topic packaging
- wrong platform
- weak first-screen clarity
- topic not compelling enough for public distribution

## 2. High click + low read-through

Possible causes:

- title / cover over-promised
- context was delayed
- opening lacked legibility
- body rhythm was too heavy

## 3. Decent read-through + weak save / share

Possible causes:

- useful but not quotable
- no sharp takeaway
- structure clear, but social value too low

## 4. Strong engagement + weak conversion

Possible causes:

- broad audience but low target-user match
- CTA too soft or too unclear
- content built attention but not trust

## 5. Good metrics on one platform, weak on another

Possible causes:

- same draft transplanted across platforms
- audience layer mismatch
- render mismatch

## 6. What not to do

- do not jump from symptom to one single cause
- do not assume the platform suppressed you without internal diagnosis
- do not confuse “people liked the topic” with “the execution was strong”
