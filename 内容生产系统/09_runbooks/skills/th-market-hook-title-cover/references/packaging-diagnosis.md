# Packaging Diagnosis

Use this file when a content piece underperforms and the likely issue is packaging, not topic quality alone.

## 1. Diagnose by symptom

### A. Low click-through, low read-through

Likely causes:

- weak topic selection
- vague title
- abstract cover
- no visible stake

### B. High click-through, low read-through

Likely causes:

- package over-promised
- context came too late
- hook was strong but article started too slowly
- the reader clicked for one thing and got another

### C. Decent read-through, weak spread

Likely causes:

- insight was useful but not socially legible
- no quotable lines
- no strong takeaway or share trigger

### D. Strong engagement, weak conversion

Likely causes:

- content was interesting but not identity-building
- CTA was missing or too vague
- audience was broad but not well matched to TH Capital’s target users

## 2. Repair order

When fixing packaging, do not change everything blindly.

Repair in this order:

1. title
2. opening
3. context bridge
4. cover
5. CTA

## 3. Learning output

Every packaging review should try to produce:

- what the reader likely expected
- what the article actually delivered
- where the mismatch happened
- what to test next time
